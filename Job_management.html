Post a Job

Before posting a Job , you need to add the team, assign the recruiter to the team and add the employees finally.So that your jobs will be reachable to everyone in the network.When a job is posted, the notifications are send to the members according to what the recruiter chose in the team.Job notifications are sent through email, wall updates and sms (if activated).

Edit a Job

If you want to do any modification in the Job post. You can do it by clicking the ‘Edit’ button on the top right corner of each job post.

Track application download

Track the applicants for a job posted. You can change the status manually by updating the status of the application in the ATS. This status will be updated to the referrer (employee) and the applicant in their respective account. Applicants will be notified by GCM, Email / via SMS.
