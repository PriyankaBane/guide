Upload profile photo

Upload your personal profile picture in the ‘Settings page’. So that your employees and their friends can able to recognize and it will be easy to start a professional communication with you.

Edit your profile

Personalize or update your profile in the 'Settings' page.

Manage email notifications

By default, SkilledTree enables few notifications such as Team Email notifications and Job Notifications. You can enable/disable the notifications on your own. This can be done by updating in the ‘Settings’ page.

Change password

Initially, the four letter auto-generated password will be sent to your Email ID / SMS. You can update your password in the ‘Settings’ page.

Delete your account

You can delete your account at any point of time by clicking on the link in the 'Settings' page.

Make your profile available

This feature can be used to switch on/off your profile visibility on search engines. If anyone searches your name in search engines, your profile can can listed in the search results.
