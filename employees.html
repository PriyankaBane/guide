Add Employees




Upload CSV/ODS

Make sure that you have added team in the ‘Settings’ page. Employees list can be uploaded in the form of an excel with the extension of *.xls,*.xlsx,*.csv. File should have Employee Id, Name, Email/Phone, Team.


By Manually

Employees can be added manually by uploading their Emp ID, Name, Email/Phone and Team He/She belongs to. More than one employee can be added at a time by clicking the ‘Add More’ Button.



View_Employees

If you want to move an employee from one team to another team, this can be possible by clicking the editable dropdown in the Employees' List which is right below on ‘View Employees’ section.



Assign Team

If you want to move an employee from one team to another team, this can be possible by clicking the editable dropdown in the Employees' List which is right below on ‘View Employees’ section.

Edit Emp ID

Employee ID can be edited by clicking the corresponding link in the Employee List.

Resend Credentials

You can send login credentials to the employee if the employee has not yet joined SkilledTree or not yet received the credentials by clicking the resend icon in the employees list. He/She will be receiving either an Email or SMS based on the information you had given.

Delete Employee

Employees information can be deleted from the database when he/she resigned from the job by clicking the delete icon in the List.

Send a Message

You can stay connected with any employees in the organization by clicking the chat icon.

Referral Bonus Payouts

This list shows the information of the referral candidates who got selected and the employees who referred him/her will get the referral rewards based on the company policy.



Why Social Integration

Employees can integrate to their social accounts so that whenever their team recruiter posts any job, it will be posted to their social media site automatically.
