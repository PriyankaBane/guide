Upload Resumes

You can upload the applicant’s resume along with the employee’s information who referred him/her to the referral program. This will be maintained by you and your team. Job and the recruiter have been assigned to the applicant for the further recruitment process.

Edit the tagged Skillsets

Resume repository is the place where all the resumes which were referred by the employees, friends and their friends will be saved. You can do in place edit by clicking the Skillset of any applicant in the list.

Search Resumes

Huge bunch of resumes will be warehoused in company resumes section. You can search with any of the fields in the column listed over there.
