Add employee to the network

Add employees using an email list - This option can be used to mass add your employees to the portal. You need to have an xls/xlsx/csv file with at least two columns for name and office email address in order to use this option. Add employees individually - This option should be used to add a limited number of employees to the network. For eg, a new batch of 10 has been recruited and instead of creating a spreadsheet with their details, simply add them manually.

View members in your network

Employees personal information will be listed with their corresponding team they belong to. The list shows employees, date they have been added, friends they have invited and friends joined etc., with the link to resend the credential email in case any employee did not receive the email.

Search members in your network

To search certain members in your network, you can search them by entering their name or their personal information in the Search box.
