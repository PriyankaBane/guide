Search for a job

After maximizing your network, jobs which are posted from your friends’ companies can be visible in your jobs wall.You can search the jobs either by its title or key skills.

Apply for a job

You can apply to any of those jobs which are posted in the wall. You can refer your friends also by uploading their friend’s resume.

Track your applications

Jobs which are applied by you can be trackable in the ‘Track your application’ page along with the resume status.

Share jobs on social media

Your department jobs have been posted in your job’s wall. You can share it via social media or by sending an email to your friends by writing an email in your email client which you are using.

Automatically share jobs on social media

Integrate your accounts with the social media. So when the recruiter posts any job ,it will be posted in your social media wall such as Facebook and LInkedIn.

Track your referrals

Your friends resume which was referred by you will be notified to you. You can track your friend’s resume status in the ‘track your referrals’ page .
