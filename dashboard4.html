Current Jobs

In order to get more jobs, you need to connect with your friends as much as you can. So whenever their company posted any jobs, you will be getting those jobs in your ‘Current Openings’ page.

Search network jobs

You can search any of the jobs which are posted by the members in your network whom you have connected with.

Search public jobs

Not only inside your network, You can search the public open jobs in SkilledTree which is available on the top left Sidebar.

Connections

Total number of friends you have connected with.

Social Media share

You can share this job to any of your social media account such as Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, Google+ and Pinterest. Your friends too can apply for those jobs you have shared.

Upload Resume

In order to apply to the job on behalf of your friend, you can apply it by uploading their resume. Automatically, SkilledTree will be created for them. Login credentials will be sent to their Email / SMS will be sent.

Apply a Job

You can apply to the any job by clicking on the Job Title. It will redirect to the new tab along with the Job description.
