Features

The following are the major functionalities that the friends can be opted for.

Add Friends

Employees can add their friends by uploading employee(csv/xls) or by adding employee manually or by integrating Gmail,Yahoo & Hotmail contacts.

Apply for a job

He can apply to a job refer his friends by uploading their resumes to the job he applied for.

Application Tracking System

His status of the application can be notified by him via email Gem notification whenever it is updated by the concerned recruiter

Stay Connected

Friends can connect with other professionals on their network by maintaining the professional relationship with them.They can expand their network by accepting the friend request.
