Add Departments

‘Default’ team is added when you create your organization account in SkilledTree. You can add a maximum of 3 Team. If you want to add more than the limit, you can send a request to SkilledTree Admin by clicking on ‘Add Team’ button in the ‘Settings’ page.

Assign Recruiter

Recruiters should be added and assigned to a particular team before posting a job. So while posting a Job, recruiters can be assigned to the job for the further interview process.

Fix referral reward

The Main purpose of employee referral is to give referral rewards to the employees once their friends get joined in the company. So it’s company policy that they can decide the referral amount or no referral rewards for the jobs.This needs to be set before posting a job. It can be added in the ‘Company Profile’ under ‘Settings’ page.

No referral reward

The company should decide whether they can provide referral rewards to the employees who referred their friends to the referral program. If they don't want to provide referral reward, they can choose'No referral reward from the dropdown which is the 'Settings'page.

Dynamic referral reward

If the company decided to give the referral amount to the employees who referred their friends, they can manually enter the value of the referral amount while posting a job without selecting any option from the dropdown.

Post a Job

Once the team, recruiters and referral reward gets fixed, you can able to post a job without any hassle. Jobs will be reachable to the team you have mentioned while posting the job and to the recruiters you have assigned.Mandatory fields are marked as with asterisk(*).

Close a Job

Jobs can be closed automatically after a month from the date you posted. You can also close the jobs by clicking the ‘Trash’ Icon. in advance if you need.

Repost a Job

Closed Jobs can be reposted again with the same data you have entered before. Reposted Jobs will be reachable to the team you have mentioned while posting the job and to the recruiters you have assigned.

Saved Jobs

You can draft a job before posting it. Those Drafted jobs will be listed here and can be posted again at any point of time.
