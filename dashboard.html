Add Team

Before getting into SkilledTree, Add your teams in your company. This can be done in the ‘Settings’ page.


Number of Job posts

Number of Job posts can be decided by SkilledTree. If you need more count, you need to contact SkilledTree for further payments.


Enabling SMS.

SMS will be enabled by SkilledTree. You can write an Email to us. While adding employees, you might need to have the maximum SMS count. SMS won’t be sent if the count is zero.


Jobs Added

Total number of jobs posted by the HRs in your company.

Jobs Assigned

Total number of jobs assigned by the Hr Manager in your company and the total number of jobs which are posted by you.

Inactive for 7 days

If the status of the referral candidates were not updated by you for about a week those total count will appear here.

Open positions

Total number of vacant Job positions.

Resumes not tagged

Resumes which are uploaded by employees in your company but are not tagged to recruiters by the Hr manager.

Under Review

Total number of referral resumes which are under the status of ‘Under Review’.

ShortListed

After under reviewing the resumes if the candidates got shortlisted, their resume status should be updated with ‘Short Listed’.Total number of the resumes which are under the status of ‘Short listed’ will be displayed in the dashboard.

Interview

Total number of ShortListed resumes which are opted for further interview process will be interviewed by the assigned recruiters

Selected

Total number of selected candidates in the interview process

Rejected

Total number of rejected candidates in the interview process.

On Process

Total number of resumes which are updated by the recruiters in the status of ‘On Process’.

Joined

Total number of candidates who have completed all the interview process are yet to join.

Closed Positions

Total number of filled job positions after referral candidate joined.
